Mobius Handle

Mobius Handle is organic, created as means to design an ergonomically appropriate handle in character. Clay, foam prototypes were experimented with to shape the final product. Considerations taken into account were:
- Organic (not rectilinear) - Subtractive (not additive) - Intuitive - High touch - Concavity-space pushing into forms - Convexity- forms pushing into space - Balance of inward and outward forces - Fluidity, elegance, rhythm
